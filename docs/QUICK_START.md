# Quick Start Guide

Get started with Dream Flow Creator Studio in 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Open the app
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter email and password
4. Verify your email

## Step 2: Create Your First Story

1. Go to **Generator** tab
2. Enter: "A peaceful story about a starlit night"
3. Select theme: **Calming**
4. Tap **Generate Story**

## Step 3: Edit Your Story

1. Story opens in **Editor**
2. Make any changes you want
3. Changes save automatically
4. Use formatting tools (Bold, Italic, etc.)

## Step 4: Preview Your Story

1. Go to **Preview** tab
2. See your story with media (if generated)
3. Play video or audio if available

## Step 5: Export Your Story

1. In Editor, tap **Export** button
2. Choose format: **PDF**, **Markdown**, or **JSON**
3. File downloads to your device

## That's It!

You've created your first story! Now explore:
- **Library**: View all your stories
- **Analytics**: See your creative stats
- **Generator**: Create more stories with different themes

## Next Steps

- Try different themes and parameters
- Generate stories with video/audio
- Organize stories in your library
- Export your favorite stories
